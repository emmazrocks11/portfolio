welcome to my mayhem

glad you stopped by!

One minute I'm mentally planning to travel halfway across the world to become a popstar, the next I'm daydreaming about going to fashion school in New York City. Needless to say, my fixations are ever-changing, yet somehow my love for Kidblog and Bitstrips never stopped. As it evolved from posters, to powerpoints, to Instagram posts, suddenly I'm now a design student in the University of Waterloo making dope websites and brands. I'm always drawn towards the unique story behind brand identities, and I believe that web design is an extension of those stories. I also really enjoy napping, snacking, and dressing up for no reason. I think the best way to get through life is to be thankful and romanticize like crazy.
