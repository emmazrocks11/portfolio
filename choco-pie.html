Project Overview

Our assignment was to conduct a complete brand redesign for any brand of our choice, as long as it wasn't already exceptional (I'm talking about these guys → Tropicana, Burger King, and Apple).

With anticipation and excitement, I partnered up with my classmate Rose with the goal to produce something out of the ordinary.

Choco Pie: A beloved sweet treat

One of South Korea's most popular snacks, Choco Pie consists of two soft layers of cake with a marshmallow filling, coated in a thin layer of chocolate.

The original Choco Pie was created by Orion Corporation in 1973. In 1979, Lotte Confectionery began to sell their version of Choco Pie.

At face value, Orion and Lotte's Choco Pie branding looks IDENTICAL. Orion ended up filing a lawsuit in 1999 against Lotte (which which Orion ended up losing).

Rose and I ended up choosing to focus on Lotte's Choco Pie. The aim for this brand redesign was to shift away from their similarities to Orion, and present them as a new take on the same beloved snack.
